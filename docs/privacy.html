Privacy Policy for Kspeaker

Last updated: December 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Kspeaker collects the following information to provide our English learning service:

  • Voice Data: Audio recordings of your speech for AI processing and feedback
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, session duration, and feature interactions
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Registration Data: Email address (if you choose to register)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your voice recordings and provide AI-powered feedback
  • Improve our speech recognition and feedback algorithms
  • Analyze app performance and fix bugs
  • Send you important service updates (if registered)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your voice recordings are:

  • Processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers
  • Transmitted securely using encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Never shared with third parties for marketing purposes

4. Third-Party Services

Kspeaker uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For natural language processing and conversation generation
  • Sentry: For crash reporting and error monitoring (anonymous)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain:

  • Voice recordings: Processed in real-time, not stored permanently
  • Usage analytics: 90 days
  • Crash logs: 30 days
  • Account data: Until you request deletion

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Export your usage data

7. Children's Privacy

Kspeaker is suitable for users aged 4 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@kartezya.com

10. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
