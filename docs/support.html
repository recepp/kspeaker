Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Kspeaker?

Simply tap the microphone button and start speaking in English. Our AI will listen and provide instant feedback on your pronunciation and grammar.

Do I need an internet connection?

Yes, Kspeaker requires an internet connection to process your voice and provide AI-powered feedback.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Please see our Privacy Policy for details.

How can I report a bug?

Please email us at support@kartezya.com with details about the issue.

Technical Support
